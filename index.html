Tetris Instruction

Controls:

Use arrow keys to move tetrominoes (←, →, ↓) and rotate them (↑).

Press "Start" to begin.

"Pause" (Esc) halts the game; "Start" resumes.

"Restart" resets game.

Starting:

Press "Start" to begin.

Moving Tetrominoes:

Arrow keys move tetrominoes; ↑ rotates.

Tetrominoes fall until the bottom or collision.

Completing Lines:

Filled horizontal lines disappear. Each line cleared earns points.

  • 1 line: 10 points
  • 2 lines: 20 points
  • 3 lines: 50 points
  • 4 lines (Tetris): 100 points

Game Over:

Stack reaching the top or no space ends the game.

Enjoy the game!
